brat summer 4ever:

BRAT's second life through its visionary remix album

By Meagan Hughes

What makes a remix album? Is it simply heavier beats, chopped vocals, and buzzy guest features? Or can it be something more?

After releasing BRAT, the viral album that wrought 2024's "brat summer" zeitgeist, Charli XCX returned only 4 months later with a thoroughly original and inspired remix album.

Brat and it's completely different but also still brat is an extension of the volatile, yet vulnerable BRAT universe.

What is the symbiotic relationship between these two albums?

365 feat Shygirl is a remix of 365, off of the original BRAT album.

It also samples Frequency by Altern 8, a 1992 UK rave banger.

365 samples the core melody of 360, the ubiquitous opening track of BRAT.

365 also reaches beyond the library of BRAT to sample forever from Charli's 2020 lockdown mixtape, how i'm feeling now.

What about the Club classics remix ft Bb trickz?

It doesn't just remix Club classics — it also samples 365, which tangles up Club classics with forever and 360 via the transitive property.

The original Club classics gets its iconic "right now" adlib from a 2010 interview with teenage Charli XCX for Rankin's Destroy.

Club classics was produced by Charli's now-husband, George Daniel of The 1975, who may have unearthed the nostalgic interview.

Through sampling and remixing, Brat and it's completely different but also still brat compounds BRAT's musical motifs into a self-referential world.



which brat is more popular?


Symbiosis aside, album versus album, the winner is clear: BRAT's total album plays since release are over double that of Brat and it's completely different but also still brat, according to LastFM.

Track versus track, the remix album pulls its weight in play counts, especially on songs with featured guests that have large fandoms (Lorde, Billie Eilish, Troye Sivan, and Ariana Grande).

Guess feat Billie Eilish and Girl, so confusing feat Lorde are two songs where guest features add heightened drama to the existing narrative arc of the original tracks. The Guess remix, which has nearly double the plays of any song in the brativerse, layers an already raunchy, voyeuristic bop with Billie seducing Charlie — and Charlie flirting with queerness.

The Girl, so confusing feat Lorde remix builds on the original track's exploration of the isolation and anxiety of girlhood. Lorde's explosive verse that briefly broke the internet restores the alliance between Charli and Lorde while throwing a gauntlet against the music industry.

when is a feature more than a feature?
